What's a jailbreak?

Jailbreaking your device means installing a small program that removes restrictions in the default software. removing restrictions in the default software. It's the only way to get third-party apps, like those in Cydia, that have been rejected by Apple or include functionality that Apple doesn't allow.

Is it safe?

Jailbreaking is completely safe. The process is completely reversible: if you ever want to remove the jailbreak, just restore your device in iTunes.

Is it legal?

Yes! The US Copyright Office ruled that jailbreaking your device is legal, so you're completely safe.

JailbreakMe is the easiest way to free your device. Experience iOS as it could be, fully customizable, themeable, and with every tweak you could possibly imagine.

Safe and completely reversible (just restore in iTunes), jailbreaking gives you control over the device you own. It only takes a minute or two, and as always, it's completely free.
